# Tech Tavern Story

## Who We Are

Tech Tavern is a sole proprietor tech consultancy run by me, Scott Turnbull. Before consulting, I led data and AI initiatives for both public sector agencies and nonprofits, where I learned that the best technology solutions are the ones that people actually want to use. I hold a PMP certification and am a Google-certified Gen-AI Leader.

When I'm not working with clients, I'm writing about tech strategy at Tech Tavern and mentoring early-career technologists in the nonprofit sector.

### Mission Statement

Helping mission-driven organizations harness AI and data responsibly — with strategy, ethics, and measurable impact.

### Vision Statement

To make advanced, responsible AI and data strategy accessible to every organization striving to make the world better.

### Core Values

- **Integrity:** We do what’s right, not just what’s easy. Every project reflects honesty and ethical stewardship.  
- **Clarity:** We make complex technology understandable, actionable, and transparent.  
- **Responsibility:** We ensure AI and data solutions are deployed safely and ethically.  
- **Impact:** We focus on measurable results that amplify social, environmental, and organizational good.

### Brand Story

Tech Tavern was founded by Scott Turnbull after two decades of leading AI, data, and cloud initiatives across nonprofit, academic, and enterprise sectors. Built on a belief that technology should serve people, not overwhelm them, Tech Tavern helps mission-driven organizations apply intelligence responsibly and confidently.

We bridge the gap between innovation and understanding — translating emerging AI capabilities into meaningful, measurable outcomes. Our name reflects what we stand for: a welcoming space where complex ideas are shared clearly, and smart solutions are crafted collaboratively.

### Brand Positioning

**What we do:** We help organizations design and implement responsible AI and data strategies that deliver measurable impact.  
**Who we serve:** Nonprofits, foundations, and public sector teams striving to use technology for good.  
**How we're different:** We combine enterprise-level technical expertise with human-centered design and ethical governance.  
**Brand promise:** Build with Intelligence. Amplify Your Impact.
